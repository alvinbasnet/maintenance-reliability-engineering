# Engineering Maintenance & Reliability Support

## Overview
This project documents engineering maintenance and reliability
support activities performed during a Highway Operations
Internship at 407 ETR.

## Objectives
- Support safe and reliable operation of highway infrastructure
- Assist in preventive and predictive maintenance activities
- Improve system uptime through data-driven analysis

## Scope of Work
- Field inspections of electromechanical systems
- Performance monitoring and condition assessments
- Maintenance documentation and reporting
- Collaboration with cross-functional teams

## Methodology
- Conducted structured inspections following safety protocols
- Collected technical field data to identify performance trends
- Supported troubleshooting and maintenance planning
- Applied preventive maintenance principles to reduce failures

## Engineering Skills Applied
- Reliability engineering concepts
- Data analysis and trend identification
- Technical documentation
- Safety-focused engineering practices

## Results
- Improved understanding of large-scale infrastructure systems
- Enhanced maintenance planning and execution
- Reinforced importance of safety and reliability in engineering

## What I Learned
- Real-world application of maintenance engineering
- Importance of reliability and safety in regulated environments
- Effective communication within multidisciplinary teams
